you have to buy towers and place them near the road for them to attack the bloons, all of them do different things and they have upgrades witch will help you in later rounds,
there are also heros in the game wich you can only place one but they can be powerful attackers or usefull supports there are different difficultys and gamemodes, odyssey mode, and races, there is also a trophie shop and co-op mode.
